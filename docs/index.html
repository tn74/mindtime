MindTime

Need to grind? Let MindTime schedule your workday for peak efficiency

What We Do

MindTime will read your google calendar for projects and assignments you need to complete. When you set aside work time on your calendar, MindTime will schedule what you need to work on to stay on track. You focus on the work. MindTime will keep you on track.

Google Calendar Integration

Keep your project and assignment deadlines on Google Calendar. MindTime will search for events that are marked with [MT] to schedule them in your work day.

Focus

Research shows we work best when we focus for short amounts of time and take breaks in between. MindTime will schedule in breaks for you so you get the most out of your workday.

Analytics

Login to the MindTime website to check your progress. Be warned early if you are behind schedule..

Private

MindTime will never store any of your event data. It will read from and post to your calendar and be done with your data.

Data Usage Policy

Mindtime takes your privacy seriously.

We Will

  • Read events on your calendar marked with [MT] in the header
  • Analyze those events and store information about what deadlines you have and how much time you've allotted to them
  • Post new events to your calendar

We Will Not

  • Read events that you don't mark as for us
  • Store info that is irrelevant to scheduling
